About Me

Hello! I'm Ma'am Razel, a passionate Social Studies teacher at San Miguel National High School in Leyte. For the past 7 years, I’ve been teaching Grade 8 Araling Panlipunan, helping students explore Philippine history, government, economics, and our country’s role in the world.

I believe that Social Studies is not just about memorizing dates and facts — it’s about understanding who we are, where we came from, and how we can shape a better future. I strive to make my classes engaging, relevant, and full of heart.

I love creating a classroom environment where students feel heard, respected, and inspired to learn. Teaching isn’t just my job — it’s my vocation.

“A good teacher can inspire hope, ignite the imagination, and instill a love of learning.”
